# Guía Visual de Cortes Vacunos y Prompts

**Referencia de Calidad para Generación de Imágenes**

Esta guía contiene 10 cortes esenciales. Para cada uno se incluye una descripción técnica de lo que buscamos (para el Shopper), una imagen de referencia generada, y el **Prompt de Ingeniería** asociado.

---

## 1. COSTILLAR (Entero)

![Costillar Entero](images/costillar_raw_1767901103232.png)

- **Qué buscamos:** Estructura de hueso grande, carne roja cubriendo el hueso, capa de grasa blanca cremosa externa. No demasiada grasa amarilla.
- **Prompt:**
  > **Professional food photography of a massive raw beef rib rack (Costillar) sitting on a dark rustic wooden table. The meat is fresh, deep red, with a thick layer of creamy white fat on top. The bones are visible and cleanly cut. Cinematic lighting, soft shadows, 8k resolution, highly detailed texture, raw meat close-up, butcher shop atmosphere.**

## 2. TIRA DE ASADO (Corte Banderita)

![Tira de Asado Banderita](images/tira_de_asado_raw_1767901118630.png)

- **Qué buscamos:** Tiras finas (1-2cm) con huesitos pequeños. Mucha carne entre hueso. Grasa infiltrada (marmoleado).
- **Prompt:**
  > **High-angle close-up of raw beef short ribs sliced thin (Asado Banderita style), laid out on a marble countertop. Bright red meat with excellent marbling and thin strips of white fat interspersed. Fresh, juicy texture. Studio lighting, sharp focus, 8k, photorealistic, gourmet ingredient presentation.**

## 3. VACÍO

![Vacío Entero](images/vacio_raw_1767901134699.png)

- **Qué buscamos:** Pieza grande sin hueso. Color rojo oscuro intenso. Debe tener la "telita" (membrana) plateada intacta en ambos lados.
- **Prompt:**
  > **A whole slab of raw Vacío (Flank Steak) meat resting on a butcher block. The meat is a rich deep red color, covered by a distinct translucent silver membrane (silverskin). Lean muscle texture with minimal fat on the inside but a fat cap on the edge. Strong contrast, dramatic lighting, ultra-realistic, moisture visible on surface.**

## 4. ENTRAÑA

![Entraña](images/entrana_raw_1767901149657.png)

- **Qué buscamos:** Pieza larga y fina. Color rojo muy oscuro (casi bordó). Grasa externa blanca. Se valora si se ve la membrana.
- **Prompt:**
  > **Raw Skirt Steak (Entraña) displayed on a slate plate. The meat is long, flat, and intensely dark red, almost burgundy. The texture shows coarse muscle fibers. Some patches of white creamy fat and silverskin on the surface. Professional food styling, macro detail, soft directional lighting, 8k, extremely fresh.**

## 5. BIFE DE CHORIZO (Striploin)

![Bife de Chorizo](images/bife_de_chorizo_raw_1767901164253.png)

- **Qué buscamos:** Corte grueso (bife). Una capa de grasa blanca en el borde superior (1-2 cm). Centro rojo y magro.
- **Prompt:**
  > **A thick, raw New York Strip steak (Bife de Chorizo) cut. Side view showing the thick layer of solid white fat on the top edge. The meat center is bright red and solid. Freshly cut, placed on a wooden cutting board with rosemary sprigs nearby for scale. Commercial food photography, studio light, vivid colors.**

## 6. OJO DE BIFE (Ribeye)

![Ojo de Bife](images/ojo_de_bife_raw_1767901189358.png)

- **Qué buscamos:** EL rey del marmoleado. Tiene que tener el "ojo" de grasa en el medio y vetas blancas por toda la carne.
- **Prompt:**
  > **Top-down view of a premium raw Ribeye steak (Ojo de Bife). Incredible marbling, with intricate webs of white fat running through the bright red muscle. A central kernel of fat is visible suitable for grilling. Luxurious quality, highly detailed texture, wet surface reflection, 8k, award-winning food photography.**

## 7. MATAMBRE

![Matambre](images/matambre_raw_1767901203934.png)

- **Qué buscamos:** Muy fino y ancho. Color rosado pálido (no rojo oscuro). Grasa blanca muy fina cubriendo una cara.
- **Prompt:**
  > **A thin, broad sheet of raw Matambre (Rose meat) spread out on a table. The color is pale pink to light red. One side has a thin, smooth layer of white fat. The texture is fine-grained. Clean, fresh appearance, soft lighting to highlight the delicate color, high resolution.**

## 8. PICANHA (Tapa de Cuadril)

![Picanha](images/picanha_raw_1767901217990.png)

- **Qué buscamos:** Forma triangular. Una capa de grasa blanca GRUESA (2cm) cubriendo toda la parte superior. Carne roja limpia abajo.
- **Prompt:**
  > **A whole raw Picanha (Sirloin Cap) roast sitting on a dark surface. Distinguishable triangular shape. A very thick, perfectly white fat cap covers the entire top side. The meat below is deep red and lean. Close-up, emphasis on the contrast between the white fat and red meat. Studio lighting, 8k.**

## 9. LOMO

![Lomo](images/lomo_raw_1767901232257.png)

- **Qué buscamos:** Cilindro perfecto. Carne muy magra (sin grasa), color rojo uniforme y suave. Olor a limpio.
- **Prompt:**
  > **Whole raw Beef Tenderloin (Lomo) cylinder, cleaned and trimmed of all fat. The meat is extremely lean, consistent dark red color, with a velvet-like smooth texture. Sitting on a clean metal surface. Minimalist composition, sharp details, high contrast, symbol of premium quality.**

## 10. OSOBUCO

![Osobuco](images/osobuco_raw_1767901247243.png)

- **Qué buscamos:** Rodaja de pierna con el hueso en el centro. El hueso tiene que tener el tuétano (caracú) dentro. Carne oscura alrededor.
- **Prompt:**
  > **Three slices of raw Osso Buco (Beef Shank) arranged overlapping. Center feature is the round white bone with rich marrow inside. Surrounding meat is dark red with connective tissue. Rustic setting, 8k resolution, highly detailed texture of the bone marrow and meat fibers.**
