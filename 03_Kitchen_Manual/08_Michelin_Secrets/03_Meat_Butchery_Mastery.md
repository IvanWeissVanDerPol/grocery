## üêÇ BEEF: HINDQUARTER (Cuarto Trasero)

### 1. PICANHA (Tapa de Cuadril)

- **The Spec:** The Queen of the BBQ. Triangular cap muscle.
- **Butchery (Trim):** Leave the fat cap ON (1cm max). Trim silverskin from the _bottom_ (meat side) completely.
- **Technique A (Fast): Reversed Sear.**
  - Score fats in diamond pattern. Bake at 110¬∞C until internal temp 48¬∞C. Sear fat-side down in cold pan -> High heat until crispy. Slice against grain.
- **Technique B (Skewers): Brazilian Style.**
  - Cut into "C" shaped steaks. Fold fat outside. Skewer. Rotisserie salt roast.
- **Pairing:** Farofa or Chimichurri.

### 2. NEW YORK STRIP (Bife de Chorizo)

- **The Spec:** The long muscle (Striploin). Flavor balance.
- **Butchery (Trim):** Remove the "chain" (side muscle) for grinding. Trim back fat to 0.5cm.
- **Technique A (Fast): Butter Baste.**
  - Pan sear high heat. Flip. Add butter, garlic, thyme. Spoon foaming butter over steak for 3 mins.
- **Technique B (Aging): Dry Age (Fridge).**
  - Place whole loin on rack in fridge (uncovered) for 3-5 days. Pellicle forms (trim it). Flavor intensifies to nutty/blue cheese notes.
- **Pairing:** Bearnaise Sauce.

### 3. TENDERLOIN (Lomo / Lomito)

- **The Spec:** Zero work, melt in mouth texture, low flavor.
- **Butchery (Trim):** Remove "Chain" (use for burgers). Remove "Silverskin" (tough white strip) carefully. Separate "Head" (Chateaubriand) from "Tail" (Tournedos).
- **Technique A (Fast): Sear & Roast.**
  - Sear hard. Finish in oven. Do not overcook (Rare/Medium Rare only).
- **Technique B (Raw): Tartare.**
  - The only cut for tartare. Hand cut 2mm cubes. Mix with capers, shallot, egg yolk, tabasco.
- **Pairing:** Bordelaise (Red Wine Reduction).

### 4. RUMP / INSIDE ROUND (Cuadril / Nalga)

- **The Spec:** Large lean muscles. Hard working.
- **Butchery (Trim):** Separate capping muscles. Remove glands.
- **Technique A (Fast): Schnitzel (Milanesa).**
  - Pound thin (tenderize). Flour-Egg-Panko. Fry in Tallow. Michelin tip: Souffl√© the breading by swirling hot oil over top.
- **Technique B (Raw): Carpaccio.**
  - Freeze log. Slice paper thin on machine. Plate cold.
- **Pairing:** Lemon wedges & Parsley butter.

## üêÇ BEEF: FOREQUARTER (Cuarto Delantero/Econ√≥mico)

### 5. CHUCK ROLL (Aguja)

- **The Spec:** Marbled, complex connection of muscles. The "Poor Man's Ribeye".
- **Butchery (Trim):** Seam butcher (separate muscles by following fat lines). The "Denis steak" (Chuck Eye) lies here.
- **Technique A (Fast): Chuck Eye Steak.**
  - Isolate the 'Eye'. Cook exactly like a Ribeye. 50% cost.
- **Technique B (Slow): Pot Roast.**
  - Cube edges. Brown hard. Braise in red wine/stock for 4 hours. Pulls apart.
- **Pairing:** Root vegetable puree.

### 6. BRISKET (Pecho)

- **The Spec:** Pectoral muscle. Tough collagen, massive flavor.
- **Butchery (Trim):** Separate "Point" (fatty) from "Flat" (lean). Trim hard fat cap to 6mm.
- **Technique A (Slow): Smoked (Texas Style).**
  - Salt & Pepper Rub. Smoke at 120¬∞C until "stall" (70¬∞C). Wrap in paper. Cook to 93¬∞C (probe like butter).
- **Technique B (Brine): Corned Beef / Pastrami.**
  - Cure in pink salt brine for 7 days. Boil (Corned Beef) or Smoke (Pastrami).
- **Pairing:** Mustard & Pickles.

## üêñ PORK (Cerdo Entero)

### 7. PORK BELLY (Panceta)

- **Butchery:** Remove ribs (Spareribs). Score skin if keeping, or remove skin for chicharr√≥n.
- **Technique (Slow): Confit Squares.**
  - Cook submerged in lard for 4 hours. Press flat in fridge overnight. Cut cubes. Deep fry before serving. Crispy skin, melting center.
- **Technique (Cure): Home Bacon.**
  - Cure with salt/sugar/pink salt 7 days. Smoke warm. Slice.

### 8. PORK SHOULDER (Paleta/Bondiola)

- **Butchery:** Bone out shoulder blade (Scapula). The "Coppa" (Bondiola) is the neck muscle.
- **Technique (Slow): Pulled Pork.**
  - Dry rub. Oven 150¬∞C for 6 hours. Shred.
- **Technique (Steaks): Pork Secreto.**
  - Find the hidden flap muscle inside shoulder. Sear fast. Best cut on the pig.

## üêë LAMB/GOAT (Cordero/Cabra)

### 9. LEG (Pierna)

- **Butchery:** Remove lymph node (musk gland) between muscles. Tunnel bone (remove femur) for easy carving.
- **Technique (Slow): 7-Hour Lamb.**
  - Garlic, Rosemary, White Wine. Spoon-tender.
- **Technique (Fast): Butterfly.**
  - Open leg flat. Grill over coals like a steak. Pink center.

### 10. RIBS (Costillar/Rack)

- **Butchery:** French the bones (scrape clean). Remove cap fat.
- **Technique:** Roast whole rack. Cut chops after cooking. Herbed crumb crust.

## üêî POULTRY (Pollo)

### 11. WHOLE CHICKEN

- **Butchery:**
  - **Breasts:** Remove bone-in (Airline) or boneless.
  - **Legs:** Separate thigh/drumstick. Or debone leg whole (Ballotine).
  - **Wings:** Tips to stock. Flats/Drums for frying.
  - **Carcass:** Stock pot immediately.
- **Technique (Breast): Sous Vide.**
  - 62¬∞C for 1 hour. Juicy guarantee.
- **Technique (Legs): Confit.**
  - Salt cure 24h. Cook in duck/chicken fat 3 hours. Store in fat.
- **Technique (Skin): Chips.**
  - Stretch flat on tray. Bake between two trays (pressed). Chicken glass.
