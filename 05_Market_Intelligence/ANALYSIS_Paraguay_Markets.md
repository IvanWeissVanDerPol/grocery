## 1. Markets (Mercados) in Paraguay

### Primary Sourcing Locations

| Market | Type | Specialty | Notes |
|:-------|:-----|:----------|:------|
| **Mercado de Abasto** | Wholesale Market | Bulk produce, meats, spices | Main sourcing location - best prices, fresh goods |
| **Mercado de San Lorenzo** | Regional Market | Alternative wholesale | Secondary option |
| **Stock** | Supermarket Chain | Pantry staples, condiments | For packaged goods |
| **Superseis** | Supermarket Chain | Pantry staples, condiments | For packaged goods |

### Market Characteristics

**Mercado de Abasto (Central Wholesale Market):**
- Best prices for bulk purchases (wholesale pricing)
- Fresh meats, fruits, vegetables, and bulk spices
- Requires early morning visits and vehicle transport
- Can carry 20-25kg bags
- Working conditions: Heat, strong odors, traffic

**Supermarkets (Stock/Superseis):**
- Better for packaged goods, condiments, and pantry staples
- Brands available: Knorr, Maggi, Hellmanns, Savora, Arisco, etc.
- Higher prices than Abasto but more convenient
